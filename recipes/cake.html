Cake

Description

Indulge in the rich, velvety goodness of our Decadent Chocolate Cake—a perfect treat for any occasion. Made with premium cocoa, this moist and fluffy cake is layered with smooth, creamy chocolate frosting for a melt-in-your-mouth experience. Whether you're celebrating a special moment or simply satisfying a sweet craving, this chocolate masterpiece is sure to delight.

Ingredients

  • 265 g All Purpose Flour
  • 55 g Cocoa Powder
  • 1.5 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1.5 tsp Baking Soda
  • 440 g White Sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 Eggs
  • 250 ml Milk
  • 125 ml Vegetable Oil
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 250 ml Boiling Water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F(160°C Fan).
  2. Grease 2 x 22cm/9" cake pans with butter, then line the base.
  3. Sift flour, cocoa, baking powder and baking soda into a large bowl. Add Sugar and salt. Whisk briefly to combine.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. Whisk well to combine until lump free.
  5. Add boiling water and whisk to incorporate. The batter is very THIN.
  6. Pour batter into cake pans.
  7. Bake for 35 minutes or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.
  8. Cool for 10 minutes, then turn out onto wire racks upside down.
  9. Cool completely before frosting.
