Cookies

Description

Satisfy your chocolate cravings with a batch of chewy, rich chocolate cookies that offer the perfect balance of sweetness and depth. These cookies have a soft center with slightly crisp edges, delivering a delightful contrast in texture with each bite. The intense cocoa flavor is complemented by melty chocolate chunks, creating a treat that’s truly indulgent and utterly irresistible.

These cookies are easy to prepare, making them an ideal option for both novice bakers and seasoned pros. The dough comes together quickly and bakes into beautifully soft cookies that fill your kitchen with the inviting aroma of chocolate. Perfect with a glass of milk or as a standalone treat, these cookies are sure to become a favorite for any chocolate lover.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3-4 tablespoons water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa

Steps

  1. Cream margarine, oil, sugar and 1 T water until light and fluffy.
  2. Mix in remaining 2-3 T of water and beat well.
  3. Mix dry ingredients.
  4. Add to margarine mixture and mix until combined.
  5. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to an hour.
  6. Preheat oven 400°F (200°C) and grease a cookie sheet well.
  7. Make 24 balls (can also do drop cookies).
  8. Bake for 10 minutes, should be very puffy.
  9. Allow to cool for 5 minutes on baking sheet, they will fall considerably due to the lack of egg.
